Politics to business, cinema to art: South Conclave 2023 to have it all | Begins soon

The two-day India Today Conclave South 2023 kicks off today, where political leaders and renowned artists will express their views on a wide range of topics pertinent to southern India.

By India Today News Desk: The India Today Conclave South 2023 starts on Thursday, June 1, where top political leaders and renowned artists will express their views on issues pertinent to southern India. The line-up for the two-day event includes Kerala Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan, Rajya Sabha members P Chidambaram and KC Venugopal, Tamil Nadu BJP president K Annamalai, MP and AIMIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi, actors Kamal Haasan, Rana Daggubati, Sobhita Dhulipala, Aishwarya Lekshmi and other famed personalities including champion sailor Abhilash Tomy, singer Vijay Yesudas and Isro Chairman S Somnath.
