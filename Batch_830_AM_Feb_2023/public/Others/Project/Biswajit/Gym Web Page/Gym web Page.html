About Us

What is a Always Fit Gym?
Always Fit is a unique structured fitness system that focuses on strength and conditioning. It features high-intensity workout that changes every day.
A CrossFit session is usually held in a Always Fit gym or a “box”. This is the dedicated place where people who sign up meet for the sessions. A Always Fit Gym Setup offers the different equipment needed for the workouts.
Often seen as hard core, Always Fit involves a coach taking people at all levels of fitness through a set of defined workouts that includes strength building exercises, the mandated workout of the day(WOD) and other heavy duty training, all done with a high level of intensity.
In terms of benefits, a CrossFit workout is said to burn 12.3 calories per minute, along with other benefits like better balance, flexibility, power and stamina. The focus is to become better physically all round and not just stronger or faster.
