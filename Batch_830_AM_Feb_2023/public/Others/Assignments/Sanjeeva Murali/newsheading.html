Breaking News 1

Hyderabad: Search operations were carried out by fire and police personnel on Friday morning to check if anyone was still trapped in the fire hit Swapnalok Complex in Secunderabad. The victims who died of asphyxiation in the fire on Thursday night were identified as Pramila, Shravani, Prashanth, Triveni, Shiva and Vennela, all aged below 25 years. The premise was closed for shop owners. Even after repeated requests from the owners of the shops on the ground and first floors of Swapnalok Complex, they were not allowed inside.
